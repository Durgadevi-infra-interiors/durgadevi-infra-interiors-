About Durgadevi Infra & Interiors

Durgadevi Infra & Interiors is a civil construction, architectural, and interior solutions company delivering residential, commercial, and turnkey projects across Karnataka. We specialize in construction, 2D & 3D design, interiors, renovation, modular kitchens, false ceilings, flooring, painting, plumbing, electrical works, and customized project execution. Driven by quality, innovation, and precision, we create modern, functional, and sustainable spaces with professional execution and lasting value.

Our Story

Established with a vision to provide complete construction and interior solutions under one roof, Durgadevi Infra & Interiors has successfully delivered projects with a commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction. We combine engineering expertise, creative design, and skilled execution to transform ideas into reality.
